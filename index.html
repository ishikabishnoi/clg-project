    About DoveTail!

    It started with a pretty simple observation, studying wasn't the hard part. Finding everything you needed to study was. The right playlist for a subject, notes, PYQs, all scattered across different drives, groups and links. Every semester, before the actual studying could begin, there was this whole search mission that nobody signed up for. DoveTail is our answer to that. One platform where students can access notes, lectures and question papers, all in one place. Built by students who faced the same problem, for students who still do.

      Why DoveTail?

        In craftsmanship, a dovetail is the strongest way to join two pieces into one unbreakable whole. We chose this name because we tried doing the same for your studies- we take your scattered resources and your schedule, locking them into one seamless, high speed workspace.

          Future Updates:

            • AI Study Assistant- From generating personalized study plans to answering doubts and summarizing topics, an integrated AI built strictly for academic purposes
            • Search Bar- Find notes, lectures and PYQs instantly without scrolling through everything.
            • Student Chat Room- A space for students to discuss, collaborate and help each other.
            • Feedback Portal
